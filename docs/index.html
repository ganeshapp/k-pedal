Why K-Pedal

Built like a paper map.
Just digital.

No backend. No subscription. No login. Just the data you need to plan a ride across Korea — bundled into a 9 MB app.

Offline-first

Routes, checkpoints, distances, and elevation profiles are all baked into the app. No signal needed at the booth.

Privacy-first

K-Pedal never asks for your location or your identity. No tracking, no analytics, no cloud calls. Stamps live on your phone.

Stays out of the way

Use Kakao or Naver Maps for navigation. Use Strava or Garmin for recording. K-Pedal just gives you the paths.

Features

Plan, ride, stamp.

The three things you actually do, given the care they deserve.

Every route, embedded.

Each path opens to a real OpenStreetMap view with the full polyline and numbered red-booth pins — exactly where they are on the trail. No tile downloads required.

  • Total distance and per-leg distance between checkpoints
  • Pre-computed SRTM elevation profile, scrollable
  • Tap any pin to jump to the checkpoint detail

Know the climbs before you go.

Every meter of elevation has been pre-computed once via Open-Topo-Data SRTM 30 m and shipped offline inside the app. No surprises on the road.

  • Total ascent and descent for the whole path
  • Profile spans the entire route, not just sampled points
  • Same data offline as online

The red booth, found.

Each checkpoint has photos of the actual booth, the official Kakao Map link, GPS coordinates, and a one-tap "Find Nearby" for lodging, stores, toilets, repair, bus, and train.

  • Sticky toggle to set Kakao or Naver as your default
  • Photos so you don't ride past the booth
  • Categorical "Find Nearby" anchored at the checkpoint

Your stamp passport, going digital.

Tap once at every red booth to collect a stamp. Complete a path to earn a gold medal. Complete all 10 to unlock the Korea Grand Slam.

  • One persistent passport across all paths
  • Stamps stored locally — no account, no sync
  • Per-path grids and a global completion view

Install

Get K-Pedal on your phone.

Currently available as a sideloaded Android APK or a build-from-source iOS install.

Android

Grab the APK that matches your phone from the GitHub Releases page. You may need to enable "Install from unknown sources".

FileDevice
arm64-v8aMost phones (2016+)
armeabi-v7aOlder phones
x86_64Emulators / Intel
Download latest release

iOS / Build from source

Flutter 3.29+ and Android SDK or Xcode. Standard Flutter workflow — clone, fetch, run.

git clone https://github.com/ganeshapp/k-pedal.git
cd k-pedal
flutter pub get
flutter run
# release APKs split per ABI
flutter build apk --release --split-per-abi
